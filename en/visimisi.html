Vision

"TO CREATE A GENERATION OF MUSLIMS WITH SOUND ISLAMIC KNOWLEDGE, EQUIPPED WITH ACADEMIC COMPETENCE, SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY, FAITH AND PIETY, AS WELL AS NOBLE CHARACTER, SO THAT THEY BECOME A SUCCESSFUL AND BLESSED GENERATION."

Mission

  • To produce students with strong faith, broad knowledge, noble character, independence, creativity, and achievements.
  • To integrate the fundamentals of religious and general sciences into a unified system.
  • To educate students to master the basics of knowledge (language, mathematics, and writing) as a foundation for academic competence.
  • To instill good morals (akhlaq karimah) in students for family and community life.
  • To cultivate closeness and love for the Qur’an and Sunnah.
  • To instill love and gratitude in students towards Allah SWT, the Creator.
  • To foster a love for the homeland and nation.

We believe that through these principles, students will be well-prepared academically, spiritually, and socially to contribute positively to society.
