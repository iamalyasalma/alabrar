Eid Adha 2025

Al-Abrar’s First Qurban

Eid Adha 2025

Eid Adha 1446H (Al-Abrar’s First Qurban). Alhamdulillah, with the permission of Allah SWT, Al-Abrar successfully held its first qurban on June 6, 2025. After the slaughtering process, all teachers, parents, and students worked together to cook and share the meals. The atmosphere was filled with blessings, a strong sense of family, and togetherness. May Allah SWT accept our qurban and make us more obedient, caring, and grateful servants.

Kelulusan TP 2024/2025

May this milestone be the beginning of a brighter future, filled with knowledge, good character, and devotion to Allah SWT.

Syahadah Tahfiz, Completion of Iqra’ & Al-Qur’an

Congratulations to the students

Syahadah Tahfiz, Completion of Iqra’ & Al-Qur’an

Alhamdulillah, with the permission of Allah SWT, Al-Abrar successfully held the Syahadah Tahfiz, Completion of Iqra’ & Al-Qur’an ceremony. This event was a special moment to celebrate the achievements of our students who have completed their journey in Iqra’ and memorization of the Qur’an.

The ceremony was filled with joy and gratitude, attended by teachers, parents, and fellow students. May Allah bless their knowledge, strengthen their faith, and make them among those who love and live by the Qur’an.

The excitement of the Independence Day competitions at Al-Abrar School.

Independence Day Competitions 2025

Al-Abrar Islamic Tahfiz Elementary School held a flag ceremony to commemorate Indonesia’s Independence Day on August 17, 2025. With great respect, all students, teachers, and staff joined the ceremony as an expression of love for the homeland and gratitude for the sacrifices of the heroes. “Independence is not only about the past, but also our responsibility to preserve and uphold it for the future.” ✨
